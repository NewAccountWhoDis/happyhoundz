C — Neo Pop

Teal + golden yellow + bubblegum pink + black
Bold black outlines, neo-brutalist card borders, playful sticker-style elements. High contrast with maximum personality. Animations feel like stickers coming to life.

Fonts: Bebas Neue + Poppins · Vibe: bold, graphic, sticker-shop energy

My recommendation: B — Electric Garden. The violet-to-pink-to-amber gradient is instantly distinctive in the Albany market, reads as both fun AND trustworthy (important for pet owners), and gives the most room for Next.js animation work that won't look gimmicky. A is the boldest risk; C is fun but can feel more like a skate shop than a groomer.
