Multi-Page Architecture

5 pages — each with its own hero, layout pattern, and animation personality. Nav links across all pages. Nothing here feels like a scroll-down-forever template.

🏠

/ — Home

The energy page. Full-height animated hero with floating paw prints + bold headline. Scrolling trust bar. 3-card services preview (teaser, links to /services). "Why Heather" 4-block section. 3 cherry-picked review quotes. Big CTA banner → /book.

Animation: headline words animate in one by one · paw prints float up on load · CTA button pulses

✂️

/services — Services & Pricing

Page hero with coral background + scissors/paw illustration. Full grid of all services organized in categories: Baths · Full Grooms · A La Carte · Add-ons. Each card has icon, name, description, and "Starting at $XX" placeholder. Sticky sidebar or tab filter on desktop.

Animation: cards stagger-animate in as you scroll · price badge bounces on hover

📸

/gallery — The Houndz Gallery

Yellow-background page with bold "Meet the Happy Houndz 🐾" header. Masonry grid of dog photos — stock placeholders tagged by breed type, easy to swap for Heather's real Facebook photos. Lightbox on click. Optional: dog name captions pulled from reviews (Nova, Kahlo, Remi, Louie, Gunner, Truffles, Chuckles).

Animation: photos fade + scale in on scroll · lightbox slides between images

🐾

/about — Meet Heather

Dark (near-black) page background for contrast — makes the coral/yellow pop harder. Split layout: Heather photo left, bio right. Section below: "The Happy Houndz Promise" with the 4 differentiators expanded into real paragraphs. Full reviews carousel at bottom — all 7 real review quotes.

Animation: photo slides in from left · text reveals word by word · reviews auto-scroll

📅

/book — Book an Appointment

Coral hero banner: "Ready for a Happy Houndz Glow-Up? 🐾". Two columns: left = big phone number + hours + address + Facebook link. Right = contact form (Name, Phone, Dog Name, Breed, Service, Preferred Date/Time, Notes). Success state with animated paw print confetti on submit.

Animation: form fields animate in on load · submit button wiggles · confetti on success

Navigation: Sticky top nav on all pages — logo left, links right (Home · Services · Gallery · About · Book). On mobile: hamburger menu slides in from right with coral background. "Book Now" nav button always styled as a bold pill CTA in yellow.
